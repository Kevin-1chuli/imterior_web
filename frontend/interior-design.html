How We Work

A structured process that starts with listening and ends with a space you actually want to live in.

1

Discovery & Brief

We meet at your space to understand how you live, what you need, and what's not working. You tell us your budget and timeline. We ask questions and take measurements.

Week 1
2

Concept Design

We develop 2–3 design directions with mood boards, floor plans, and material palettes. You choose the approach that feels right, and we refine it together.

Week 2–3
3

Detailed Design & 3D

We produce technical drawings, 3D renders, and a full specification document covering every material, fixture, and finish. You review and approve before we order anything.

Week 4–5
4

Procurement & Build

We source materials, build custom furniture in our workshop, coordinate contractors, and manage the entire build process. You get regular updates and site visits.

Week 6–12
5

Installation & Handover

Our team installs everything, handles final styling, and walks you through your new space. We provide care instructions and remain available for any adjustments.

Week 13–14
