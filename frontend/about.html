Built on One Conviction

Great design should be accessible, personal, and built to last.

2018

How We Started

NGB Interiors was founded in Kampala with a simple mission: to make exceptional interior design and custom furniture accessible across Uganda. We started in a small workshop, building pieces by hand and learning what it means to craft something that lasts.

Our first project was a dining table for a family in Kololo. They wanted something specific—a size that would fit their space perfectly, a finish that matched their home, and joinery they could trust. We built it, delivered it, and watched them gather around it. That's when we knew this was the work we wanted to do.

2020

Growing the Team

As demand grew, we brought on artisans, designers, and project managers who shared our approach: listen first, design second, build to the exact standard promised. Every person on our team knows how to use their hands and how to solve problems. We don't outsource the hard parts.

Today

200+ Spaces Transformed

We've completed over 200 projects—homes, offices, retail spaces, and restaurants—across Uganda and East Africa. Every project begins with a conversation about how you live and what you need. Then we design it, build it, install it, and stand behind it.

We're still in that same workshop in Kampala, just bigger now. The work hasn't changed: we make furniture and spaces that feel like they've always belonged there.
