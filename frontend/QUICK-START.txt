🎨 TO UPDATE PRODUCT INFORMATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Edit furniture.html in any text editor:

1. CHANGE PRICE:
   Line ~230: <span class="product-spec__value">UGX X,XXX,XXX</span>

2. CHANGE DESCRIPTION:
   Line ~220: <p class="product-detail__description">Your text...</p>

3. CHANGE IMAGE:
   Line ~210: <img src="../assets/images/gallery/your-image.jpg" />

4. CHANGE FORM OPTIONS:
   Lines ~250-300: Edit <option> tags in <select> dropdowns
